Scenes:
Choose your starter Pokémon (Bulbasaur, Charmander, or Squirtle)
Explore Route 1 to battle and catch wild Pokémon
Visit the Pokémon Center to heal your Pokémon
Shop at the Poké Mart to buy Poké Balls and Potions
Check your Backpack for nice information
Battle the first Gym
Battle your Rival
Find Secret Endings

Major Decisions:
Pick your Starter
Choice after First Gym


Nice Features:
Track your stats (coins, catches, heals, faints)
Multiple endings based on your performance
Randomized encounters and damage for replayability


Endings
Ending Requirements
Secret Ending	Have 100+ coins and at least 1 Poké Ball before quitting.
Good Ending	Have 80+ coins when quitting.
Bad Ending	Faint 3 or more times.
Neutral Ending	Quit without meeting the above conditions.

Tips
Use Potions wisely and BUYING potions will be helpful — they restore 10 HP.
You can run from battle, but success isn’t guaranteed.
Catching pokemon does not mean you can use them
Save up coins to unlock the secret ending!
Random events make each playthrough different.
